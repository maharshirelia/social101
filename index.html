After a lot of brainstorming, meditating, and coffee-overdosing, he chalked out the plan for Social 101. And hence, we came into existence on 4th June 2018 to solve all your small and big marketing problems.

Social 101 is a full-service interactive media agency. We are a team of content creators, graphic designers, web developers, SEO experts and digital media strategists who serve brands beyond their imaginations. We at Social 101 aim to help your business grow substantially and organically with the latest technologies & minds at work.

Services

Services

THINGS WE DO TO MAKE YOU GREAT

BRANDING

If you need branding, we will create a consistent visual identification of your service or product. Since we believe branding is an essential part of your communication strategy, we work closely with you to ensure a solid foundation for your brand. If you already have it, we will work on designs following your brand guidelines.

SOCIAL MEDIA MARKETING

At Social 101, we develop a consistent messaging & visual strategy for your brand to foster deeper connections with your audience. Not only will we help develop a foundation for your social media accounts, but we’ll develop designs for everyday engagement on your digital platforms.

WEBSITE DESIGN & DEVELOPMENT

We aim to convert potentials to actual customers by developing a visually appealing website for your brand that strengthens your credibility & exaggerates your capabilities. Trust us to deliver the best and workable website design possible for you just so you give off enough information to attract the right people to your brand. Not too much, not too less.

CONTENT MARKETING

We create content which is valuable, relevant and consistent to attract, acquire and retain your target audience. The content which will drive profitable customer reactions. We develop infographics, webpages, videos, podcasts, blogs, and everything else your business need to build a huge fan base for itself.

STRATEGIC CONSULTING

Building a company starts with the strategy, being a very integral part. Strategy defines the ‘who’. ‘what’, ‘where’, ‘why’ and ‘how’ of the brand. We at Social 101 focus on sleek and smart strategy to help the brands not only enter but maintain the spot in the consumer space.

VIDEO MARKETING

We offer assistance in video production to clients from startups to enterprises, with a focus on advertising and marketing. We create videos to help your brand tell its story in an interactive manner. We develop corporate intros, campaign videos, ad films, and product demos. We try to be inventive in every video we create for our clients.
